## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Audit preparation achievable in ≤ 2 days
- [ ] ≥ 95% of evidence collected automatically
- [ ] Policy compliance rate ≥ 99%
- [ ] SOC 2 Type II controls mapped and monitored
- [ ] ISO 27001 controls mapped and monitored
- [ ] On-demand audit package generation operational
- [ ] Annual audit cost reduction ≥ 60% validated
