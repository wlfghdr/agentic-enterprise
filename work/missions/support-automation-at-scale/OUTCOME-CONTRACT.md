## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Auto-resolution rate ≥ 60%
- [ ] Median first response time ≤ 60 seconds
- [ ] Customer satisfaction ≥ 4.5/5
- [ ] Escalation accuracy ≥ 90%
- [ ] Multi-channel support operational (email, chat, portal)
- [ ] Human handoff seamless with full context transfer
